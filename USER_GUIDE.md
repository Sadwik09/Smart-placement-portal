## üë®‚Äçüéì Student Guide

### Getting Started

#### Step 1: Register
1. Visit the portal homepage
2. Click "Register" button
3. Select **"Student"** as your role
4. Fill in your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email (institutional email recommended)
   - Phone Number
   - Password (min 8 characters)
5. Click "Register"
6. You'll be redirected to login page
7. Log in with your email and password

#### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Profile
2. Fill in your academic details:
   - **Roll Number**: Your unique ID
   - **Branch**: CSE, ECE, ME, etc.
   - **Year**: Final year or previous
   - **CGPA**: Current cumulative GPA
   - **Skills**: Technical skills you possess
   - **Bio**: Brief about yourself
   - **LinkedIn**: Your LinkedIn profile URL
   - **GitHub**: Your GitHub profile URL
   - **Portfolio**: Your portfolio website (if any)
3. Click "Save Profile"
4. Your profile is now visible to recruiters

### Browse and Apply for Jobs

#### Finding Jobs
1. Navigate to **"Jobs"** menu
2. Use filters to narrow down:
   - **Location**: City or remote
   - **Job Type**: Full-time, Internship, etc.
   - **Skills**: Required skills
   - **Salary Range**: CTC requirement
3. Click on job card to view full details
4. Check:
   - Job description
   - Required skills
   - Salary, location, experience
   - Application deadline

#### Applying for Jobs
1. On job details page, click **"Apply Now"**
2. A modal will appear
3. Write your **cover letter** (0-500 characters)
4. Click **"Submit Application"**
5. You'll see a success message
6. Track application in **"My Applications"**

#### Application Status
- **Applied**: Your application submitted
- **Reviewing**: Recruiter reviewing
- **Shortlisted**: Selected for interview
- **Interview Scheduled**: Date and time shared
- **Selected**: You got the job! üéâ
- **Rejected**: Application not selected

### Resume Management

#### Upload Resume
1. Navigate to **"Resume"** tab in dashboard
2. Click "Choose File" or drag-drop PDF
3. Select your PDF resume
4. Click "Upload"
5. System automatically:
   - Parses your resume
   - Extracts skills
   - Generates score (0-100)
   - Shows recommendations

#### Understanding Your Score
Your resume gets 7-factor scoring:

| Factor | Weight | Meaning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Skills | 20% | Technical skills mentioned |
| Experience | 20% | Previous work experience |
| Education | 15% | Degree and qualifications |
| Formatting | 15% | Document layout and clarity |
| Keywords | 15% | Industry relevant terms |
| Length | 10% | Optimal 1-2 pages |
| Contact Info | 5% | Email, phone, links |

**Score Interpretation:**
- 90-100: Excellent üåü
- 80-89: Very Good ‚≠ê
- 70-79: Good ‚úÖ
- 60-69: Fair ‚ö†Ô∏è
- <60: Needs Improvement üìù

#### Resume Tips
1. Use industry-standard format
2. Include all relevant skills
3. Add quantifiable achievements
4. Keep to 1-2 pages maximum
5. Proofread for errors
6. Add LinkedIn/GitHub links
7. Use ATS-friendly formatting

### AI Recommendations

#### Get Job Recommendations
1. Click **"Recommendations"** in menu
2. System shows jobs ranked by match
3. Each job shows:
   - Match Score (%)
   - Matched Skills (green tags)
   - Missing Skills (orange tags)
   - Job details

#### Understanding Match Score
- **90-100%**: Excellent match - apply now!
- **70-89%**: Good match - you're qualified
- **50-69%**: Fair match - learn missing skills
- **<50%**: Limited match - not ideal

#### How to Improve Recommendations
1. Upload updated resume with more skills
2. Add skills to your profile
3. Take courses for missing skills
4. Complete projects showcasing skills

## üíº Recruiter Guide

### Getting Started

#### Step 1: Register
1. Click "Register" on homepage
2. Select **"Recruiter"** as role
3. Fill in details:
   - Your Name
   - Email (company email)
   - Phone
   - Company Name
   - Company Website
   - Password
4. Click "Register"
5. **Wait for Admin Approval** (usually 24 hours)
6. Check email for approval notification

#### Step 2: Company Profile
Once approved, complete:
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Company Profile
2. Add:
   - Company Description
   - Industry Type
   - Company Size
   - Headquarters Location
   - Logo/Banner
   - Social Media Links
3. Save Profile

### Post Jobs

#### Create Job Posting
1. Click **"Post Job"** button
2. Fill in **Basic Information**:
   - Job Title
   - Company Name
   - Job Type (Full-time/Internship/Part-time)
   - Location
   - Toggle: Remote work option
3. Fill in **Description**:
   - Job description (what they'll do)
   - Company background
   - Team information
4. Fill in **Requirements**:
   - Required Skills (add multiple)
   - Experience (years)
   - Education Level
   - Minimum CGPA
   - Additional Qualifications
5. Fill in **Salary & Deadline**:
   - CTC (Cost To Company)
   - Application Deadline
6. Click **"Post Job"**

#### Job Posting Tips
1. **Clear Title**: Use standard job titles (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
2. **Detailed Description**: Describe role, responsibilities, team
3. **Specific Skills**: List exact technologies/skills needed
4. **Realistic Requirements**: Don't ask for unrealistic experience
5. **Competitive Salary**: Research market rates
6. **Early Deadline**: Give students time to apply (usually 30 days)

### Manage Applications

#### View Applications
1. Click **"My Jobs"** in menu
2. Select a job
3. See all applications received
4. Filter by:
   - **Status**: Applied/Shortlisted/Interview/Selected/Rejected
   - **Date**: Recent/Oldest
   - **Rating**: Best matches first

#### Review Applications
1. Click on application to view:
   - Student resume (download available)
   - Cover letter
   - Student profile
   - AI Match Score
2. Use AI recommendations to identify top candidates

#### Change Application Status

**Flow:**
```
Applied ‚Üí Shortlisted ‚Üí Interview Scheduled ‚Üí Selected
                                          ‚Üì

```

To change status:
1. Open application

4. Student receives notification automatically

### Schedule Interviews

#### Schedule Interview
1. Select application with status "Shortlisted"
2. Click **"Schedule Interview"**
3. Fill interview details:
   - **Date**: Pick a date
   - **Time**: Pick a time
   - **Mode**: Online/Offline/Phone
   - If Online: Add meeting link (Zoom/Teams/Google Meet)
   - If Offline: Add location/address
   - **Interviewer**: Your name
   - **Interviewer Email**: Your email
   - **Notes**: Any instructions for candidate
4. Click "Schedule Interview"
5. Student gets notification with all details

#### Interview Best Practices
1. **Clear Communication**: Provide all details beforehand
2. **Professional Setup**: Ensure good internet for video calls
3. **Punctuality**: Join on time, start on time
4. **Technical Check**: Verify video/audio 5 min before
5. **Documentation**: Take notes during interview
6. **Follow-up**: Send feedback within 24 hours

## ‚ùì FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! We use:
- SSL/HTTPS encryption
- JWT authentication
- Password hashing with bcrypt
- Data backup daily
- GDPR compliant

**Q: Can I change my role after registration?**
A: No, role is fixed. Create a new account if needed.

**Q: Who can see my contact information?**
A: Recruiters can see if you apply. You control profile visibility.

**Q: How often is data backed up?**
A: Daily automated backups at 2 AM.

### Student Questions

**Q: How many jobs can I apply to?**
A: Unlimited! Apply to as many as you want.

**Q: Can I withdraw my application?**
A: Yes, go to "Applications" and click "Withdraw".

**Q: What if my resume score is low?**
A: See recommendations section. Update resume with:
- More specific skills
- Quantifiable achievements
- Better formatting
- More keywords

**Q: When will I hear back about my application?**
A: Usually within 1-3 weeks. You'll get notifications.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my profile?**
A: Yes, notifications show when recruiters view your profile.

**Q: How do I prepare for interviews?**
A: In "Interviews" section, see "Prepare for Interview" tips.

### Recruiter Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Usually 24 hours, max 48 hours.

**Q: Can I post multiple jobs?**
A: Yes, unlimited jobs can be posted.

**Q: How can I find the best candidates?**
A: Use our AI recommendations - candidates ranked by match score.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my job posting?**
A: Yes, analytics show views and applications.

**Q: How do I reach out to candidates?**
A: Schedule interviews or send messages through the platform.

**Q: Can I set custom application questions?**
A: Not yet, but coming soon!

### Admin Questions

**Q: How do I reset a user password?**
A: Go to Users ‚Üí select user ‚Üí "Reset Password".

**Q: Can I delete users?**
A: Yes, but it removes all their data. Use deactivate instead.

**Q: How do I handle complaints?**
A: Go to "Complaints" section, review, and take action.

**Q: Can I see salary negotiations?**
A: No, we don't track salary discussions (confidential).
